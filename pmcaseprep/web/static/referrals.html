🤝 Referral Paths

find the people who'd actually refer you

A referral multiplies your interview odds. You already know the people — close friends, college batchmates, old teammates — this tool just surfaces them from your own LinkedIn data, grouped by how you know them.

🔒 Your connections never leave this page. The file is read entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored on any server. Close the tab and it's gone.

1 · Get your LinkedIn connections file

  1. On LinkedIn: Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Get a copy of your data
  2. Pick “Want something in particular?” → Connections and request the archive
  3. LinkedIn emails you a download link (usually within ~10 minutes)
  4. Unzip it and drop Connections.csv below

Why a file and not a “Connect LinkedIn” button? LinkedIn shut down its connections API years ago — no app can read your network directly anymore. The official export is the one ToS-clean way, and it keeps your data in your hands.

Drop Connections.csv here — or click to choose the file

2 · A few quick facts (better grouping)

The export lists each person's company and when you connected — but not where they studied. These answers unlock smarter buckets, starting with the one that matters most: who you know at the company you're targeting.
